# Outputs

## Accepted Outputs

No accepted outputs exist yet.

This is deliberate. The pack is scoped, not solved.

## Candidate Output Types

- Evidence inventory.
- Dataset inventory.
- District-level contamination risk map for one to three SSA countries.
- Climate suitability variable review with back-test results.
- Biocontrol and post-harvest intervention evidence review.
- Failure-mode review.
- Field-reality review.

## Publication Rule

Published outputs must say what they are not. A contamination risk map is not a food safety emergency declaration, a trade restriction recommendation, or a clinical treatment guide.
