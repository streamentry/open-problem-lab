# Outputs

## Accepted Outputs

No accepted outputs exist yet.

## Candidate Types

- Source inventory separating usable, limited, and rejected stillbirth sources.
- Measure-family reconciliation note blocking invalid stillbirth comparisons.
- Country-feasibility memo for follow-on verification work.
- Short claim note distinguishing when apparent stillbirth burden points first to care-quality verification versus counting-system strengthening.
- Red-team review on misuse of facility stillbirth rates and perverse reporting incentives.
- Field-reality review with maternal-newborn quality-improvement or CRVS workflows.

## Publication Rule

Published outputs must say what they are not.

- A high facility stillbirth rate is not proof of poor clinical performance.
- A low registered stillbirth count is not proof of low burden.
- A survey estimate is not interchangeable with a CRVS stillbirth rate.
- A country shortlist is not a league table for blame.
