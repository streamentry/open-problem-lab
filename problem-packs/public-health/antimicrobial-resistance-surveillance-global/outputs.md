No accepted outputs exist yet.

## Candidate Output Types

- Evidence inventory with source classification.
- Dataset inventory with grain, license, and limitation documentation.
- GLASS data compilation with sub-national reporting status.
- Laboratory-capacity map.
- Surveillance-gap analysis comparing burden to coverage.
- Red-team review of misuse scenarios.
- Field-reality review for lab-network planning.

## Publication Rule

Published outputs must state what they are not. A surveillance-gap map is not a lab-investment recommendation, an antibiotic-access restriction, or a national AMR policy directive.
