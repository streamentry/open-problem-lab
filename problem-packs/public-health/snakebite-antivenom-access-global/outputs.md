# Outputs

## Accepted Outputs

No accepted outputs exist yet.

## Candidate Output Types

- Evidence inventory with source classification.
- Dataset inventory with grain, license, and limitation documentation.
- Sub-national snakebite risk map combining incidence, agricultural population, and habitat proxies.
- Antivenom access-gap map comparing risk to facility-level stocking and travel-time.
- Red-team review of misuse scenarios.
- Field-reality review for snakebite program planning.

## Publication Rule

Published outputs must state what they are not. An antivenom access-gap map is not a procurement recommendation, a clinical guideline, or a national snakebite policy directive.
