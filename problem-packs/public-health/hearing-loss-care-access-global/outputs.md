## Candidate Output Types

- Failure-mode review of allocation framing.
- Field-reality review of ministry decision utility.

## Publication Rule

Published outputs must say what they are not. A coverage gap estimate is not a clinical recommendation, a hearing aid deployment order, or a health ministry policy directive. It is evidence to inform a decision, not the decision itself.
