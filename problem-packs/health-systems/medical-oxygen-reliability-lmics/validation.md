# Validation

## Validation Layers

| Layer           | Gate                                                                            |
| --------------- | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Structure       | `pnpm validate` passes schemas and pack completeness checks                     |
| Evidence        | Every quantified statement has a dated source and explicit limitation           |
| Definitions     | Reliability, function, quality, and delivery are not conflated                  |
| Reproducibility | A reviewer can rerun classifications from documented fields and rules           |
| Review          | Domain, field-reality, and red-team reviewers approve high-risk interpretations |
| Replication     | Quantitative outputs are independently reproduced before publication            |

## Baseline Requirements

Any facility-level analysis must report:

- Sampling frame and facility level.
- Observation window and measurement mode.
- Numerator, denominator, missingness, and exclusions.
- Component-level results for source, power, distribution, delivery, monitoring, quality, and maintenance where available.
- Non-use cases, especially the distinction between system reliability and patient outcomes.

## Failure Rule

If a field cannot distinguish ownership from function or a survey cannot expose its denominator and observation window, it cannot enter a canonical reliability estimate. The record may remain as limited evidence.
