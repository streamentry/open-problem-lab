# Evidence Ledger

The machine-readable ledger is `evidence.json`.

## Evidence Notes

- **WHO access statement:** Strong global framing for the existence of an uninterrupted-access gap and its multi-component causes. It does not justify a country ranking.
- **Ijaz et al. 2025:** The strongest current facility-level benchmark located for this pack. Its percentages are useful for measurement calibration, not a representative estimate for every LMIC or a causal estimate of mortality.
- **WHO oxygen technical resources:** Establish the system vocabulary needed to avoid the common error of treating a delivered device as equivalent to a working oxygen service.

## Evidence Quality Rule

No source is treated as proof of patient benefit or investment return unless it measures that outcome with an appropriate design. Facility equipment presence, reported reliability, oxygen concentration, and patient-level delivery remain separate evidence fields.
