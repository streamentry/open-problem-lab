# Evidence Register

The machine-readable evidence register is `evidence.json`. Each record states one claim, its source date, access date, assessment method, and limitations.

## Evidence Boundary

The initial evidence establishes burden and a plausible measurement gap. It does not establish a universal undercount correction, a causal safety intervention, or a safe basis for automated enforcement.

## Review Requirement

Before a city-specific linkage result is accepted, a reviewer must verify the source systems, matching method, duplicate handling, missingness, subgroup coverage, privacy controls, and whether the result changes a named decision.
