# Playbooks

## Analyst Playbook

Start with `problem.json`, `evidence.json`, and `tasks.json`. Classify the processing activity before classifying the risk. Preserve the distinction between a candidate cluster, a measured exposure, a health outcome, and an intervention effect. Document missing informal activity rather than treating formal records as the denominator.

## Reviewer Playbook

Ask: What exact activity was observed? Which media or biomarker was measured? At what grain and date? What alternative sources explain the signal? Does the result justify sampling, or is it being overextended into enforcement or health advice?

## Field-Reality Playbook

Name the decision-maker, decision, timing, affected workers and residents, livelihood dependence, enforcement pathway, and non-use cases. Treat closure or relocation as a potential harm and require evidence that the intervention reduces exposure rather than moving it.
