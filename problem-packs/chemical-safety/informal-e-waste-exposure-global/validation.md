# Validation

## Validation Layers

| Layer           | Gate                                                                                            |
| --------------- | ----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Structure       | `pnpm validate` passes schemas and problem-pack completeness checks                             |
| Evidence        | Every burden or pathway statement links to a dated evidence record                              |
| Reproducibility | Cluster or exposure artifacts include rerunnable inputs, transformations, and missingness rules |
| Domain review   | Chemical-safety or environmental-health reviewer checks source and measurement interpretation   |
| Safety review   | Red-team reviewer checks enforcement, displacement, stigmatization, and false-reassurance risks |
| Field review    | Named decision-maker, decision, timing, and non-use cases are documented                        |
| Replication     | Quantitative or spatial claims are independently rerun before acceptance                        |

## Cluster Requirements

Any candidate-cluster artifact must document:

- Processing activity and observation date.
- Evidence that distinguishes e-waste processing from general waste or formal recycling.
- Geographic grain, coordinate uncertainty, and activity status.
- Data missingness and likely under-counting of informal activity.
- False-positive and false-negative review on a declared sample.

## Exposure Requirements

No map or proxy may be called an exposure measurement. Any measured claim must document medium or biomarker, analyte, detection limit, sampling frame, quality controls, chain of custody, ethics status where relevant, and uncertainty. Proximity alone is insufficient.

## Intervention Requirements

No output may recommend closure, relocation, worker screening, remediation, or formalization as effective until it names the counterfactual, potential displacement, livelihood effects, implementation pathway, and independent replication status.
