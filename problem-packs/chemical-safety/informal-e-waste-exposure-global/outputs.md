# Outputs

## Accepted Outputs

None yet.

## Candidate Types

- Dated source inventory.
- Candidate processing-cluster inventory with provenance and uncertainty.
- Exposure-pathway matrix separating proxy indicators from measured media.
- Replicated site-level evidence assessment.
- Red-team review of enforcement, displacement, stigmatization, and false reassurance.

## Publication Rule

A candidate-cluster map is not an exposure map. A measured concentration is not a health effect estimate. A health association is not proof that a specific enforcement, formalization, relocation, or remediation action will work. No output may recommend site closure, relocation, screening, or individual action without domain, red-team, field-reality, and replication gates.
