# Outputs

## Accepted Outputs

No accepted outputs exist yet.

This is deliberate. The pack is scoped, not solved.

## Candidate Output Types

- Evidence inventory.
- Dataset inventory.
- Sub-national WBGT exposure map for one to three LMICs.
- Exposure-outcome analysis with effect size and transferability assessment.
- Worker protection intervention evidence review.
- Failure-mode review.
- Field-reality review.

## Publication Rule

Published outputs must say what they are not. An exposure-outcome map is not a workplace safety enforcement directive, an employer liability determination, or a clinical treatment guide. It does not assign blame to workers or employers for heat-related injuries.
